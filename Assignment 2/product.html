Our Products

The original Snickers bar combines chewy nougat, rich caramel, and crunchy peanuts, all enrobed in a layer of creamy milk chocolate. It’s the perfect blend of sweet and salty flavors, offering a satisfying treat that's been a favorite for decades.
Snickers with Almonds replaces the traditional peanuts with crunchy almonds, adding a delightful twist to the classic candy bar. It features the same delicious nougat and caramel, all coated in smooth milk chocolate, providing a unique nutty flavor that's sure to please almond lovers.
Snickers Ice Cream Bars bring the beloved Snickers flavor to a frozen treat. This version combines creamy vanilla ice cream with ribbons of caramel and crunchy peanuts, all dipped in a chocolatey coating. It’s a refreshing and indulgent way to enjoy the classic Snickers taste.
